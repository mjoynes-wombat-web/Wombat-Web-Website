Vision

At Wombat Web Design we hope to provide you with an experience that will leave you relaxed and confident that your needs are being met. We will communicate with you every step of the way to ensure that our aspirations are your goals. Quality, integrity, service, and communication are the cornerstones of our business and we strive to provide you with the same experience we would hope for in any worthy business.

We design products that are classy and intuitive, but are still unique and memorable. With your vision and insight into your market, and our experience and understanding of customer's needs, as a team we know we can produce visuals that will bring customers to your door.

Expertise

With our experience in web, print, apparel, and sign design we can provide unique designs that maintain your company's identity across multiple media types. What looks good on a screen may not work on a shirt or a sign. Working with a range of media has given our staff the ability to ensure quality and brand identification on whatever you produce.

Staff

Simeon Smith

As our owner and lead designer, Simeon Smith provides Wombat Web Design with direction. His oversight ensures satisfaction and quality for our customers. With almost a decade in the design industry, his experience in management and design ensures not only top quality web design but also designs that can be used for a wide range of media. We are here to help you design and produce whatever products you are looking for.
